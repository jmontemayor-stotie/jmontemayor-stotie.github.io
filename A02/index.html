IPHONE XR

The iPhone XR, released in late 2018, is known as Apple's budget flagship model, offering a balance of performance and affordability. It features a 6.1-inch LCD display, a 12-megapixel single-lens rear camera, and is powered by the A12 Bionic chip. The iPhone XR is available in a variety of colors and is considered a good alternative to the more expensive iPhone XS and XS Max, providing a similar user experience with some scaled-down specifications. It is also recognized for its stylish design and solid performance, making it a popular choice among users looking for a reliable iPhone at a lower price point. For detailed specifications, you can refer to the official Apple Support page.
